Renowned for its standing in the academic world, diverse array of athletics, and status as a green oasis in Houston, Rice University is frequented by people who hold a wide spectrum of interests. Whether the individual is a world-famous speaker or a bride taking her wedding pictures, Rice is a visitor's favorite.

Rice offers many options for all parties, including the new first come first serve free lot on the north side of campus denoted by the orage car above. Both visiters and students are able to park in this lot for up to one week at no charge. Click below to learn more.
